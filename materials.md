# List of materials needed
* M4 x 35 mm countersunk screws (longer screws can also work) x3 pcs.
* 0.1 mm thick copper wire
* M4 rivert nuts with small collar x3pcs
* M4 brass threaded inserts x3pcs
* Arduino Uno board
* ADC module ADS1115
* Dupont cables
* USB-A to USB-B cable
* Superglue
* Sandpaper
* Electrical tape
* Small breadboard
* 5V DC power connectors
* Metal slinky spring 60mm in diameter
* Copper pipe reduccer 18-22 mm
* A pair of neodimium magnets similar in dimensions to the ones on the following links:
        * https://www.hornbach.ro/p/magnet-cilindric-industrial-o10x10mm-neodim-set-6-bucati/4621734/
        * https://www.hornbach.ro/p/magnet-cu-margine-de-prindere-industrial-6x10mm-set-4-bucati/10509146/
